About Me

Future astronaut, turned IE

I have known since I was 6 that I was going to be an engineer. At the time my dream was to grow up and be an astronaut just as the likes of Neil Armstrong and other Purdue Engineers had before me. But as I learned more about the different varieties of engineers, I quickly found out that my true calling was Industrial Engineering. As an IE I can make an impact on peoples' lives by improving the everyday systems in today's society.
