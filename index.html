About Me

Future astronaut, turned IE

I have known since I was 6 that I was going to be an engineer. At the time my dream was to grow up and be an astronaut just as the likes of Neil Armstrong and other Purdue Engineers had before me. But as I learned more about the different varieties of engineers, I quickly found out that Industrial Engineering was the right fit for me. As an IE I can make an impact on peoples' lives by improving the everyday systems in today's society.

During my undergraduate studies I made it a goal to experience as many facets of industrial engineering as possible - and since IEs are so versitle, this was no small feat. I gained experience in research, human factors, operations research, supply chain, simulation, and project management, but through my various experiences I found that my true calling lies in production systems.

I am now working as an Industrial Engineer in the field at a Kohl's e-commerce fulfillment center in Plainfield, IN. My job consists of process improvement, project management, and providing general support for building operations.
